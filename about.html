About {Diana}

Even though Diana Mahmoud has worked in multimedia over 20 years, she understands the frustrations of those who feel overwhelmed and even a little left behind in the digital age. “I understand not everyone grew up with an iPad because I didn't either. As much as I hate to admit it, I took a typing class instead of a keyboarding class. I also remember a time when a mouse was to be loathed, cookies were to be eaten, and browsing was done in public at the local mall.”

Despite this disadvantage, she built a career, first in traditional media and later in digital with a focus on customer engagement and online user experience. As an accomplished multimedia project manager with SAS’s award-winning Curriculum Pathways, a pioneering, interactive educational software, she worked on the development of cutting-edge Internet content that was at the same time user-friendly and accesible to its audience.

By the 20009, she started exploring social media's many possibilities and advantages, and began teaching small business owners to have both the knowledge and confidence to improve their online presence and customer engagement. She continues to use her experience and expertise as a certified social media marketer and web developer to help people understand how to use the latest tools to promote themselves and their professional endeavors.

Are you ready to do digital, too? Contact Diana for a consultation.
