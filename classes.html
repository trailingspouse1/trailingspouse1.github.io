Wanna Do What Diana {Does}?


Do you understand who sees what when you make a comment on a personal Facebook timeline? Are you interested in learning how to promote your business using Facebook pages? Have you ever thought about starting a blog? BeakSpeak media offers a variety of courses in Singapore that help you get started in understanding the digital world around you.

Private group courses (4 -10 students) can be scheduled and organized at convenience. For rates on individual sessions, please inquire using the contact form.

Blogging with WordPress

Have you ever thought about starting a blog, but don’t know how to begin? This 2.5 hour course will introduce you to blogging using WordPress.com—a free, hosted publishing platform.

In this class you will learn:

  • the best practices for blogging and promoting your blog
  • the difference between hosted and self-hosted blog
  • how to set up a basic, hosted blog using WordPress
  • how to make basic customizations to look of the blog, adding a custom header, pages, and widgets
  • publishing and editing posts, adding media
  • sorting articles using tags and categories
  • understanding analytics of how many people visit your blog and how they find it

Basic Facebook Marketing

The small-group, 4-hour workshop will introduce business owners to best practices of Facebook marketing from determining your social media goals to content planning and creation to measuring success.

In this class you will learn:

  • Understanding how social media marketing differs from tradition media marketing
  • Understanding key steps in creating your own social media strategy
  • Content planning - creating a content calendar to plan and align content to objectives
  • Learn where to find inexpensive, yet useful content and images
  • Using content calendar to build your Facebook page
  • Understanding Facebook advertising
  • Introduction to third-party applications
  • Introduction into Facebook analytics and measurement

E-mail Marketing Using MailChimp

Would you like to utilize e-mail as a way to promote your business, but don't know where to begin? This class will introduce you to e-mail marketing using MailChimp, a free and popular e-mail marketing tool. 

In this class you will learn:

  • Best practices to create a successful e-mail campaign
  • Laws and guidelines for e-mail marketers in Singapore
  • How to create an e-mail campaign
  • How to create and segment lists
  • How to solicit subscribers on Facebook
  • Understanding Mailchimp Analytics

Facebook 101

If you feel uncomfortable using the most popular, social-networking site on the Internet, but feel compelled to learn Facebook, this class is for you. The first 30 minutes of the course will be dedicated to helping those who do not currently have a personal Facebook page to set up one of their own. The remaining 2 hours will discuss the best practices for keeping your data secure as well as exploring the following features:

  • Editing your  profile and customizing who sees what
  • Setting privacy levels 
  • Posting  statuses, links, photographs to your timeline
  • Finding and interacting with Facebook friends
  • Understanding how to customize your News Feed so you see more of the information you want
  • Sending messages and chatting with friends
  • Best practices to keep your data secure
