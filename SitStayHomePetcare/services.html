Services

Dog Walking

Do you have a very young or very old animal who needs extra check-in visits or walks? We will happily help out. We offer morning, mid-afternoon, or evening visitng hours on a per-visit basis.

In-Home Pet Sitting

Do you travel frequently and prefer not to kennel your pet? Do you have a pet that cannot be kenneled in a traditional facility because of allergies, severe anxiety, or another condition? Would you feel more secure knowing your pet can stay in his or her home with a trusted caretaker? Do you have more than one pet and find kenneling to be too expensive? Regardless of the reason, our in-home pet sitting service is a great alternative to dog kennels.

Pet Taxi

Can't find the time to take Fido for his annual vaccinations? Do you need a ride to your groomer or veterinarian? We will happily cart you and/or your pet around town.

Special Services

Can't bear to trim your pet's nails? Need help giving your pet his or her medication? Looking for special, pet-related assistance? Contact us today to discuss your needs.
