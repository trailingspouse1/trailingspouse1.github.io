Your pet is family to us.

At Sit-Stay-Home Pet Care, we not only believe your pet is family, we treat them as if they are OUR family, too. With trained staff, ready to service your pet’s needs around the clock, you have comfort in knowing your beloved animal is in exceptional hands. And because our staff is thorough vetted and our business is liscensed and insured, you can be sure that your pet is being taken care of by only the best staff in the greater Roanoke area.

Dog care

Whether a young, active pupster or more aged and infirmed, we understand dogs have special needs at different stages of their life. With this in mind, we are happy to offer your dog the following services: in­-home pet sitting on a per-visit basis, daily walks, checkin visits (including medication administration), nail trimming and grinding, and pet taxi service.

Cat care

Your cat is special and we understand just how much you want your cat to feel secure even when you aren't there. We are happy to offer your favorite feline the following services tailored to its needs: in­-home pet sitting on a per-visit basis, checkin visits (including medication administration), and pet taxi services.

Other critters, too!

Wondering what to do with your favorite octopus? Well fret no more, friends, because we love all creatures, big and small. We happily offer pet sitting and check-in services for pets that are difficult to arrange care for such as birds, reptiles, and certain dog breeds such as wolf hybrids.
