Conditions treated:

Mary Beth and Kelley bring nearly 50 years of experience to Total Body Rehab. Their expertise enables them to treat a wide variety of patient problems including, but not limited to:

  • Postural Anomalies
  • Shoulder/Neck Pain
  • SI Dysfunction
  • Temporal Mandibular Dysfunction
  • Disorders of the Spine
  • Headaches
  • Fibromyalgia
  • Chronic pain
  • Post Surgical Rehab
  • Orthopedic extremity injuries including sprains, strains, post-surgical, total joint replacements, running injuries and other sports related injuries.

They regularly participate in continuing education programs to further their skills and ensure that their patients will benefit from the latest techniques and advances. They truly enjoy helping their patients obtain optimal health through their comprehensive approach.

Patients of Total Body Rehab can expect to receive the highest quality of physical therapy care and attention by caring, dedicated licensed physical therapists in a warm and friendly environment. We look forward to the opportunity to work with you in the healing process.
