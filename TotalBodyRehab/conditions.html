Conditions treated:

Mary Beth brings nearly 34 years of experience to Total Body Rehab. Her expertise enables her to treat a wide variety of patient problems including, but not limited to:

  • Postural Anomalies
  • Shoulder/Neck Pain
  • SI Dysfunction
  • Temporal Mandibular Dysfunction
  • Disorders of the Spine
  • Headaches
  • Fibromyalgia
  • Chronic pain
  • Post Surgical Rehab
  • Orthopedic extremity injuries including sprains, strains, post-surgical, total joint replacements, running injuries and other sports related injuries.

She regularly participates in continuing education programs to further her skills and ensure that her patients will benefit from the latest techniques and advances. She truly enjoys helping her patients obtain optimal health through their comprehensive approach.

Patients of Total Body Rehab can expect to receive the highest quality of physical therapy care and attention by a caring, dedicated licensed physical therapist in a warm and friendly environment. Mary Beth looks forward to the opportunity to work with you in the healing process.
